Reason for the refund:

We will reverse funds to the same method of payment the client has made via our system.

Card reversals can take up to 7-10 working days to reflect back to the clients card and once supplied with banking details, EFT reversals can take up to 2 working days to reflect in their bank accounts.

Card reversals can only be conducted within 6 months of the initial payment. Where any errors are experienced with returning funds to a card, we will either request bank account details or have our finance team further assist with returning failed card payments should the buyer not have South African banking details.
